    About Nest & Nosh

    Nest & Nosh started with a simple love for making people smile through handcrafted sweet treats. What began as gifts for friends and family quickly grew into a little business built on one idea: every celebration deserves something handmade and sweet.

    We hand-dip, hand-roll, and hand-decorate every order in small batches -- never mass-produced, always made with care. We use Almond Bark in place of real chocolate so that everyone can enjoy our treats.

    Allergies & Ingredients

    We want everyone to enjoy our treats safely.

    💛
    We use Almond Bark -- not real chocolate.

    All of our dipped treats use Almond Bark in place of real chocolate. This makes our treats more accessible, though Almond Bark does contain tree nuts.

    Common allergens in our kitchen

    🌰Tree Nuts (Almond Bark)
    🥛Milk & Dairy
    🌾Wheat & Gluten
    🥚Eggs
    🫘Soy
    ⚠
    Cross-contamination notice.

    All treats are made in a shared kitchen. While we clean carefully between batches, we cannot guarantee any item is fully free of trace allergens. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us before ordering.
