Our Story

A cozy corner of
sweetness & warmth

Nest & Nosh was born from a simple belief: that the best sweets are made slowly, with quality ingredients and a whole lot of care. We blend classic baking techniques with playful candy craft to create treats that feel like a hug in every bite.

From fluffy layer cakes to hand-pulled caramels and painted chocolates, everything we make tells a story — and we'd love it to be part of yours.
