our story

About Nest & Nosh

Nest & Nosh started in a tiny home kitchen with a single tray of chocolate-covered strawberries and a big love for making people smile. What began as treats for friends and family quickly grew into a little business built on one idea: every celebration deserves something handmade and sweet.

Today we hand-dip, hand-roll, and hand-decorate every order in small batches — never mass-produced, always made with care. From a dozen strawberries for date night to a full dessert table for 100, we treat each order like it's for someone we love. Because to us, it always is.

your safety matters

Allergies & Ingredients

We want everyone to enjoy our treats safely. Here's what you should know before you order.

Common allergens in our kitchen

Our treats may contain or come into contact with the following:

🥜Peanuts & Tree Nuts
🥛Milk & Dairy
🌾Wheat & Gluten
🥚Eggs
🫘Soy
🍫Chocolate
⚠️
Cross-contamination notice.

All of our treats are made in a shared kitchen that handles nuts, dairy, wheat, eggs, and soy. While we clean carefully between batches, we cannot guarantee any item is fully free of trace allergens.

Have a special request?

We'll do our best to accommodate dietary needs — just let us know in the order notes or reach out before ordering. Possible options can include nut-free batches, alternative coatings, and more, depending on availability.

💛
Please note.

This information is provided to help you make safe choices and is not medical advice. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us directly before ordering so we can talk through your needs.
